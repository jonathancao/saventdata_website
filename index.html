Industry

B2B sales and market intelligence firms leverage insights to help sales and marketing teams acheieve most outcome from their investment. The reputation of the firms is only as good as the quality of their data

In e-commerce, study showed that it only costs $1 to implement data quality check at load time, $10 to fix the data quality issues later, and $100 estimated loss without data quality check at all. It is the "1-10-100" rule. The earlier the data quality issues are addressed, the lower the cost is.

Game development in today's market has become more and more data driven. Game analytics is used to understand player behavior and increase use engagement. High quality data ensures game designers focuse on the most engaging game mechanics.

ML powered personalization is a key pillar for online entertainment providers to allow users to experience content tailored to their interests. Harvard Business Reviews cited poor data quality as #1 road block for the wide adoption of ML application.
